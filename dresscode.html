Dress Code

Formal, Black Tie Preferred

Tuxedo
Evening Gown

We would love to see everyone dressed to the nines for our evening at the palácio. Formal, black tie preferred: think tuxedos, floor-length gowns, and elegant suits.

Color and patterns are encouraged!

Above all, we want you to feel wonderful. If a tuxedo isn't in your closet, please don't rush out to buy one. Just come elegant, comfortable, and ready to celebrate.

A Few Practical Notes

Porto in early June brings warm days and mild evenings, so a light wrap or jacket is a lovely idea for after sunset.

The palácio has cobblestones and outdoor terraces, so block heels or wedges will treat you far better than stilettos.
