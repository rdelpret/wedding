Our celebration will take place at the magnificent Pestana Palacio do Freixo, an 18th-century baroque palace designed by the renowned Italian architect Nicolau Nasoni. Perched along the banks of the Douro River, this National Monument has been welcoming guests since 1910 and is recognised as a contributing site to Porto's UNESCO World Heritage status.

The palace offers breathtaking views of the Douro River and is located just 3 kilometres from Porto's historic city centre, making it the perfect setting for our wedding celebration.
